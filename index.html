About Us

Local Service is a small initiative to encourage young helpers to take on simple, safe, and responsible tasks around their neighborhood. We believe that with the right attitude and supervision, kids can develop valuable life skills and a strong sense of responsibility.

Sudhendra – A friendly local helper who started this initiative to give kids a chance to learn responsibility and earn a little money through simple tasks.
